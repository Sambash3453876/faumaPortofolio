About Fatuma

A voice for authentic African stories

Assalamu Alaikum! I'm Fatuma Hassan, a Somali-Kenyan content creator based in the heart of Nairobi. Born and raised between the vibrant streets of Eastleigh and the modern pulse of Westlands, my content bridges worlds — traditional and contemporary, East African and global.

For over 5 years I've been creating content that celebrates Muslim lifestyle, Somali culture, East African beauty, food, travel, and fashion — always with authenticity, always with purpose. My hijab is not just a covering; it's a statement of identity that shapes every visual story I tell.

I collaborate with local and international brands who want to genuinely connect with Muslim and African audiences. My community trusts me — and that trust is the most valuable asset I bring to every partnership.
